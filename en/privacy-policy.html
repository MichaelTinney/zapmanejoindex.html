Privacy Policy

Catalytic Verticals ("we," "us," or "our") operates ZapManejo, a livestock management dashboard accessible at https://zapmanejo.com (the "Site") and through the ZapManejo mobile/web application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Site, App, or interact with us via WhatsApp (Meta) or other channels.

By using ZapManejo, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Name, email, phone number (including WhatsApp), farm location, and payment details.
  • Livestock Data: Messages sent via WhatsApp (e.g., animal tags, vaccination records, production notes).
  • Support Requests: Any data shared during support.

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized trends.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session and analytics cookies (you can disable in browser).

C. Data from Meta (Facebook/WhatsApp)

  • App-Scoped User ID (ASID): Unique identifier from Meta when you connect via WhatsApp.
  • Phone Number: Used only to route WhatsApp messages.
  • Message Content: Processed to build your dashboard.

We do not store your WhatsApp chat history beyond what is needed for the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process WhatsApp messages into dashboards and reports.
  • Provide support and service updates.
  • Prevent fraud and comply with laws (LGPD, CCPA, GDPR).
  • With consent: Send marketing about ZapManejo.

4. Data Security & Retention

  • Security: TLS, encryption, access controls.
  • Retention:
    • Account data: While active + 30 days after closure.
    • Livestock data: Exportable; deleted on request.
    • Meta-sourced IDs (ASIDs): Deleted within 7 days of deletion request.

5. Your Rights & How to Delete Your Data

A. Delete Your ZapManejo Account

  1. Log in to https://zapmanejo.com
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion
  4. All data is permanently deleted within 7 days.

B. Meta/Facebook Data Deletion

  1. Go to Facebook Settings > Apps
  2. Find ZapManejo and click Remove
  3. This triggers our deletion callback at: https://zapmanejo.com/meta-data-deletion-callback
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email with a unique code and status link.

C. Manual Deletion Requests

Email privacy@zapmanejo.com. We respond within 30 days.

D. Meta Tech Provider Verification

ZapManejo uses the WhatsApp Business API under Meta's whatsapp_business_management permission. Catalytic Verticals is verified as a Tech Provider. For details, contact support@zapmanejo.com.
