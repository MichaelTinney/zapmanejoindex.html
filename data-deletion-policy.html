Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: November 4, 2025

At Catalytic Verticals, we respect your right to delete your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can permanently remove your account and all associated data from ZapManejo.

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Send an email to:

admin@catalyticverticals.com

Subject: "Request to Delete My ZapManejo Account"

Step 2: We will reply within 24 hours with a verification email sent to the address registered with your account.

Step 3: Click the confirmation link in the email.

Step 4: Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

What Gets Deleted?

  • All personal information (name, email, phone)
  • All ranch data (cattle, births, deaths, rainfall, temperatures, areas, teams)
  • Login history and activity logs
  • Any backups containing your data

This deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will not be able to recover any data.
